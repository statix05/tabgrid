Privacy Policy — TabGrid

Last updated: 2026-03-17

TabGrid does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information. All bookmark data and grid settings are stored locally on your device using the Chrome browser's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local). No information ever leaves your browser. No analytics, telemetry, cookies, or third-party services are used. The extension does not communicate with any external servers.
